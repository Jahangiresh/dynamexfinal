Summer facial skin care

09.06.2020

It is no coincidence that as we move from one season to another, we change our clothes, the accessories we use, and sometimes even the environment in which we live. Now you may think, yes, it's true, we won't wear winter boots or coats in summer! That's right, if so, don’t you need to make seasonal changes in skin care ?! What are the rules for having a healthy and fresh skin in the summer months, which are the best time to relax, but also the most difficult times for the skin ?!

 

So here's a little tip on what our blog is all about. So, let's get familiar with all the sections that will appear in our blog.

 

  • Above all, light makeup

  • It is not enough to wash your face, use tonics!

  • SPF, SPF and SPF

  • Antioxidants?!

  • Fruits are our friends!

  • Let's not forget the eye area!

  • H2O!

 

 

1) First of all, light makeup

 

 

Summer is coming, so we need to remove heavy make-up from our faces, just as we remove heavy and thick clothes from our wardrobes. For skin care, it is important to use light-textured products that allow the skin to breathe more easily.

 

It's time to release your face from heavy foundations. If you are one of those people who say that you can't go for a walk without covering your face with foundation, it is advisable to use natural foundations or BB and CC creams with a light texture, which will not create heaviness on your skin.To learn more about this, we recommend reading our blog about the types of foundations. In addition, you can achieve a more subtle coverage by using powders, over moisturizing creams with SPF for the face.

 

If you want to get BB or CC creams that you can use on summer days at a more affordable price, you can order BB and CC creams through Dynamic Express by following the link, which we have collected especially for you on Trendyol website.

 

 

2) It is not enough to wash your face, use tonics!

 

 

After make-up, you cleane your face with micellar water and then wash it with clean water, do you think your skin is clean and is ready for moisturizer?
Did you know that when we wash our face with water, the chlorine and rust in the water disturbs the pH of the skin? This is where tonics come to our aid. Using a tonic is the last step in the facial cleansing process.

 

The tonic maintains the pH balance of cleansed skin. PH balance is very important for the skin to perform its functions in a healthy way, as well as to maintain the skin's oil balance and micro flora. A well-cleansed and toned skin benefits more from serums and creams. Tonics narrow the pores of the skin, form a protective shield, increase skin moisture and nourish. Removes stubborn makeup, oil & dirt that facial cleanser can't clean

 

In short, it is a great benefit to include tonics among the products you will use for skin care in the summer. You can read our other blog to learn more about the other benefits of tonics on the skin, what tools you can use as a tonic, and tonics you can make by yourself at home.

 

3) SPF, SPF and SPF!

 

 

Burns, wrinkles, skin cancer and premature aging. Living these things aside, even reading them, evokes negative feelings in a person, right?

 

I will tell you what can cause all this. "Burning and ultraviolet sunlight."


Sunlight can be harmful to the skin at any time of the year, but it is necessary to use creams with a minimum 30 SPF , as our skin is more exposed to ultraviolet light during the summer. By using creams with SPF (Sun Protection Factor), you can get rid of the above-mentioned problems and protect your skin from the harmful ultraviolet light of the sun. Not just your face but your arms, legs, in short, your whole body needs SPF creams.

 

It is recommended to apply creams with a minimum SPF factor of 30 to 20-30 minutes before sun exposure. In addition, you should reapply your skin every 2 hours in the sun. For your convenience, we have put together SPF creams on the Hepsiburada site, there are great benefits to looking at!

 

4) Antioxidants ?!

 

 

If you were told that there is a potion that will delay the aging of your skin and prevent further damage, would you use it?

 

As we all know, as the body ages, the aging of our skin is a natural process. It is impossible to stop this process, but it is quite possible to slow down this process by using products that protect our skin and delay aging. Premature aging of our skin is one of the most worrying problems. Inflamed facial skin prevents the skin from rejuvenating. Creams and serums rich in natural extracts and antioxidants play a major role in preventing inflammation, neutralizing harmful free radicals and regenerating facial skin, especially during the summer.

 

The most well-known antioxidants are vitamins E and C. Vitamin C protects the skin from environmental damage, prevents wrinkles and promotes collagen development.

 

5) Fruits are our friend!

 

 

We must not forget that the food we eat is as important as the care products we use to have flawless skin. It is important to eat fruits and vegetables, especially in the summer months, to maintain the health and radiance of the skin. Vitamins and minerals in fruits and vegetables make the skin more elastic and delay the formation of wrinkles. In addition, they contain important vitamins and antioxidants that increase the production of collagen, reduce inflammation and protect the skin from the harmful effects of the sun.

 

You already know the function and benefits of antioxidants in our skin, and collagen is abundant in the facial skin, providing its firmness and elasticity. However, with age, the production of collagen weakens, and this is mainly reflected in the condition of the skin. Therefore, eating a lot of fruits and vegetables during the summer can partially prevent the lack of collagen in the skin.

 

6) Let's not forget the eye area!

 

 

Did you know that your eye area is thinner than other parts of your face and needs special care?
If you didn't know, you can do the care you've missed so far during the summer by paying attention to what we've written.

First and foremost, don't forget to wear sunglasses when you're out in the sun in the summer. This will prevent wrinkles around your eyes. In general, it is not recommended to go out in the sun between 12 and 4 pm in summer.

 

In addition, you can have clearer and healthier skin by using moisturizers for around the eyes, both during the day and at night, as we apply to the skin. Let's remind that Dynamic Express carries liquid products and allows you to easily order any eye care product from Turkey.

 

In your care, use products rich in moisturizing substances such as hyaluronic acid, honey and cucumber. By massaging the area around the eyes every night, you will both relieve fatigue and relax your muscles.

 

7) H2O!

 

 

Water is clarity in every subject. The most important process that can restore the moisture lost by the sun on the skin is drinking water. It is known that during the summer months, the body loses more water, and one of the most affected organs is our skin.

 

It is impossible to imagine hot summer days without water, but we do not drink water only to quench our thirst. By drinking enough water during the day, we can moisturize the skin and rid the body of toxins. Whenever possible you should have water with you when you go out in the sun during the summer months. Drink 2-3 liters of water a day to minimize the damage that the sun can cause.

And finally, do not be afraid of the Sun! Because sunlight helps us produce more serotonin, a mood-boosting hormone. Therefore, the lack of sun in the winter months can make you feel relatively sad. Let's appreciate the sun and, of course, let's not forget the ways to properly protect the skin from it. In addition, if you have summer skin care methods that you follow, you can share them with us by mentioning them in the comments section.

 

Dear readers, have a happy summer!
