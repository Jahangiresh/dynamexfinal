Turkey's online shopping websites - [2020]

08.06.2020

What are the cheapest and most discounted online shopping websites in Turkey? What are the lesser known but useful websites? If you are looking for answers to your questions, you are in the right place, this blog is written just for you.

 

One of the great features of Internet is that it is possible to shop online without leaving home. Not so long ago, 10 years ago, the possibility of such a thing could seem fantastic, but one of the indispensable things in today's world is online shopping.

 

Unlike traditional stores, online shopping websites offer customers many campaign and discount opportunities. Even so, owning one is still beyond the reach of the average person. The main question is which site is better to buy from?

In this blog, we have analyzed and collected many different categories of online shopping websites for you in detail. We will first present you the sectioning of the websites and then short and distinctive features. So, let's start:

 

I would also like to note that you can order online from any of these websites in Turkey via Dynamex. You can get information on how to order from this link.

 

Turkey's online clothing shopping websites:

Morhipo

 

 

Morhipo is an online shopping site founded by Boyner Holding in 2011 and has won the sympathy of customers over the years. Morhipo's discounts of up to 90% on brands have made it one of the most watched fashion websites in Turkey. You can also easily shop online using Morhipo's mobile app.

 

Trendyol

 

 

One of the first websites that comes to mind when ordering online from Turkey is undoubtedly Trendyol. This site even has its own brand "Milla by Trendyol". You can order goods that will suit your taste at a very low price, and you can get them quickly through the fast cargo function.

 

Tozlu

 

 

Tozlu.com, one of the alternative websites in the clothing category, was founded in 2011. This site, which is distinguished by its variety in the category of closed (Hijab) clothing, has managed to become one of the most important shopping websites in Turkey by offering daily special discount offers to its customers.

 

Sportive

 

 

As the name suggests, it is one of the online shopping websites specializing in the sale of sports goods. Here you will find not only sportswear, but also accessories for all kinds of sports. You can also order many products of well-known brands such as Nike, Adidas, Puma, New Balance, Under Armour, Skechers, Slazenger here.

 

Boyner

 

 

Perfumes and accessories can also be found on the Boyner online shopping site, which focuses mainly on clothing. The biggest difference from its competitors is the presence of an outlet inside the site.

 

Turkey’s household goods websites:

Koctas

 

 

The company, which has been in the Turkish market since 1996, opened its first online store in 2005. On the site where you can find countless home decor, kitchen and garden items, there are also products of leading European brands.

 

Evidea

 

 

Evidea online store is indispensable for those who love to shop for their home, for those who want to add decorative value to suit every taste without the need for a designer. You can follow the latest trends in home decor through this online shopping site.

 

 

 

Shopping websites with many product categories:

n11

 

 

One of the online shopping sites in Turkey is n11, which delights its customers with the big discounts it offers on the 11th of November every year. In this online store you can find many different categories of products, from household items to technological devices, from clothing to sports equipment.

 

Hepsiburada

 

 

It is one of the most popular websites for online shopping. With more than 12.7 million product assortments, you will find everything you are looking for. It should be noted that in 2013, it was selected as the best online shopping site in Turkey, gained the trust of its customers and continues to do so.

 

GittiGidiyor

 

 

Gittigidiyor, which has been operating since 2001 and is well-established among online shopping websites, has more than 23 million active users. Just as you can find goods from many different categories, you can also find second-hand products here. Thanks to the "Star Opportunities" campaign, many products range from ironing to bags.

 

 

Ideal websites for book shopping:

Kitapyurdu

It is one of the leaders in the field with a book portfolio ranging from the latest publications to the classics, covering a wide range of genres, from the philosophical and psychological genres to business and personal development. You can also find magazines and stationery at Kitapyurdu.

 

D&R

 

 

The main distinguishing feature of D&R online book shopping site, which basically serves the same sector as Kitapyurdu, is the sale of electronics, gifts and sports equipment. In addition, it is useful not to forget the frequent discount campaigns.

 

Bonus

Akakce

 

 

The online shopping websites mentioned above often offer huge discounts on depleted products. They created such a site in order not to miss the discounts that expire in a short time. However, on this site, where discount products are located, Trendyol, n11, etc. You can quickly find out about the campaigns of online stores and order a discounted product first.

 

Finally, we would like to note that we have not mentioned the brands' own websites in this blog, because we do not have the time  to post or most probably, you do not have patience to read information on each brand's website.

That's why we have included online shopping websites that have a wider product portfolio and include many brands.
