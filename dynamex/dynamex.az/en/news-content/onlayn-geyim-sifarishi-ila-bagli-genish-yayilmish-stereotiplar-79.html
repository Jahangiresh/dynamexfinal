Widespread stereotypes about ordering clothes online

16.12.2020

Although many of us want to order clothes online, the subconscious stereotypes do not allow us to take advantage of online shopping.

 

Mental categories and labels that are involuntarily formed in our minds help us in many cases. Without them, we can not justify our actions in different situations, cause-and-effect relationship, and actions we take in our daily lives. However, sometimes these mental categories form positive or negative opinions about topics that we do not even know about. If we do not try to break these stereotypes, positive or negative thoughts we think can complicate situations, because the reason for what we do or do not will not be based on solid information or facts. In our example, we will not be able to take advantage of order clothes online.

 

That's why, if you have the same misconceptions, you will see that it is unreasonable to stick these widespread stereotypes after reading this blog and there is really no reason not to order clothes online. You can trust us to deliver your package on favorable and convenient terms by registering on Dynamex.az.

 

Although buyers have different opinions, some of the stereotypes that everyone thinks before ordering clothes online are the same:

1) It is not possible to determine the correct size when ordering clothes online.

This stereotype prevents many people from taking advantage of ordering clothes online. But, of course, this is completely wrong. Because online clothes stores try to write down exactly what you might be interested in about sizes and clothes. If you still can't be sure and don't even know your exact size, then you can easily take a look at other buyers' comments or go to this blog where we talk about the subtleties of sizing when ordering clothes and shoes online.

 

 

2) Information about the quality of clothes is not written correctly.


The most important feature that interests us when ordering clothes online is its quality and durability. It is obvious that none of us want winter clothes or shoes we buy on a rainy day to be of poor quality and leave us halfway. In fact, ordering clothes online is less likely to be deceived about quality than store-bought ones. Doesn't sound real? Then let's remember a situation we have all lived in. Mostly, when we buy clothes, we buy them after sales representatives praise them a lot, but then we see that quality of the product is not same. When ordering online, this is almost impossible. Because when you use online sites, there are no sales representatives who can deceive you about their quality, all the power is in your hands. In addition, in the comments, other buyers also express their views on the quality of clothes, and again, this opportunity does not pass into your hands when you buy from the store.

 

3) Expensive products are always of better quality.


Understanding price and quality as contradictory concepts is one of the biggest mistakes and misconceptions we make when ordering clothes online. Actually, we make this mistake not only when ordering clothes online, but also when ordering from stores. However, this is even more relevant when shopping online, because we can not look at the quality of the product we want to buy on the spot. Although many shoppers subconsciously associate this behavior with the thought, "We're not rich enough to buy cheap goods," the rules of the game change completely when ordering clothes online. When shopping online, you can easily compare clothes from different stores. In this case, many buyers who see a shirt for 100 manat think that it will be better than the shirts offered for 25 manat, but this is not always the case. Although high prices sometimes indicate that the fabrics or wool used are of high quality, in many cases expensive products are expensive only in terms of brand perception and marketing. There is a lot of competition on online platforms, which is why many popular clothing brands often try to apply discounts or better serve customers with loyalty programs. Therefore, when ordering clothes online, first determine your budget, and then pay attention to material of each dress, according to this budget. Don't compare quality settings with prices, and don't forget to look at the reviews of other customers.

 

 

4) If I order clothes online, my card information can be stolen.


Some buyers think that when I order clothes online, the bank card information I registered in stores may be stolen or an additional charge may be deducted from my card without my acceptance . In fact, there is no reason for this concern.

 

First, it is possible to determine whether a website you order online is reliable by looking at a few features of the brand's website. If you don't know what to look for, you can read our list of ways to determine whether online shopping sites are trustworthy before choosing a site to order, and look at those factors to determine if the site you are ordering online from is safe.

 

Second, you now have ability to monitor all transactions on your card using improved security measures. For example, if you do not confirm the code that comes to your mobile number every time to withdraw money from your card through a 3D security code which is supported by almost all online clothing ordering brands, no funds will be deducted from your account and you will have full control over your card transactions. You can insure your account from such an unpleasant situation in a way that is not yet widely used in our country, but is very common in foreign countries. So, with this method, which is called "e-wallet", you can only add the amount you will spend to this wallet and make your payment here to prevent excess amount from your card. If you still can't trust these methods or your bank card is not valid on the website of the brand you want to order clothes online, you can easily order clothes online without using your bank card using Dynamex's "Order for Me" service.

 

 

5) Clothes I order online can get lost in the cargo or take too long to arrive.


When you order clothes online, the arrival time of your package depends on several factors. Delivery of the clothes from online store you ordered to an external warehouse may be delayed if you delay filling out declaration in your personal account and declaration in smart customs application, or if there is an error in the information. To prevent this, as Dynamex, we provide delivery from Turkey 5 days a week. Also, there is no reason to worry about this possibility as your orders are insured free of charge by us if it is included into our warehouse and packaged in accordance with international shipping rules or all products with sensitive shipping rules.

 

 

6) It is not possible to contact customer service when ordering clothes online.


When we buy clothes from stores, we try to return to the store when the need for return or any problem arises and solve the problem. Of course, the fact that we can go to the store physically does not always mean that our problem will be solved. Some buyers think that if I order clothes online, I will not be able to contact anyone if necessary. This is not the case at all, as many online shopping sites allow you to be contacted through call center and e-mail, to express your comments and suggestions. Also, if you find any problems with your clothes you can return them on more convenient and affordable terms with Dynamex's return service.

 

The main reason for these stereotypes, which many of us have, is that we do not have enough information about the situations and processes. Since we have clarified that such negative opinions are unfounded, you can easily order clothes online and use the new clothes you bought at reasonable prices on happy days.

 

Dynamex wishes each of you pleasant shopping experience!
