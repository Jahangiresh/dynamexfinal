Online shopping sites for ordering perfume from Turkey

15.09.2020

Still hesitant to order perfume from Turkey? Not sure which sites to order from? Having trouble choosing the one that suits your taste when buying perfume online?

 

Many people complain about the short-term durability of perfumes or their long absence on the skin. At the beginning of the blog, we answered questions and complaints about the use of perfume. Also, the question of which shopping sites to order affordable and high-quality fragrances will no longer bother you. First of all, let's look at what to look for when choosing a perfume. At the end of the blog, the most ideal online sites for shopping for perfume in Turkey are listed for you.

 

What to look for when choosing a perfume?

At the same time, choosing the right perfumes is just as important as choosing the right clothes for you. Perfumes have a different texture on each skin. Forget the concept of choosing a perfume according to skin color, which is a common idea.

The main nuance that affects the durability of the fragrance is the skin type. If you have oily skin type, the durability of fragrances is stronger. Dry skin will also have less lasting fragrance.

Another nuance you should pay attention to before choosing a perfume is that the fragrance is suitable for the season. It is desirable to use a fragrance with the word "winter" only in winter. On cold days, it is difficult to smell light perfumes. Therefore, it is recommended to use more pungent scents by perfumers in winter. On hot days, it is desirable to use milder scents.

It is advisable to pay attention to different types of fragrances, such as Eau de Toilette, Eau de Perfume and Eau de Cologne. The aroma base of Eau de Toilette is 5-20%, so its durability is less than that of Eau de Perfume. Eau de Cologne has an aroma base of less than 5%, so it is intended for men.

 

Now let's look at 3 main ways to use perfume:

 

  • Use on clean skin. If you apply the scent after a shower, it will be long-lasting.

  • Just don't use a perfume. If you use the same perfume every day, you will have a hard time smelling it. Therefore, it is recommended to have more than one fragrance.

  • It is not recommended to rub the perfume on the wrists and neck again after applying it. Otherwise, its durability will be short-lived.
