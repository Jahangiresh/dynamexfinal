How to choose a foundation? 12 makeup foundation types according to skin type and needs

09.06.2020

There are several secrets to a flawless makeup. The first of these is foundations. When it comes to foundations, a number of questions come to mind. How to choose a foundation? How to use? How to find a foundation for your skin type? How to decide on the color of foundation?

You will find the answers to all these questions when you read our blog. We have also researched the types of foundations for you according to your skin type and needs, and you will get acquainted with these types in the continuation of the blog. Let's start!

How to choose a foundation?

There are many answers to this question, but the best way to choose the right foundation is to check. After a few days of use, you can see if it suits you. Another method is your skin type and what you need. Oily, dry and combination skin types need different foundations. At the same time, you should decide whether you are looking for a foundation with a light texture or a concealer that hides imperfections or a different feature of your skin. 

 

For oily skin, choose powder-based and matte tones, and for dry skin, choose moisturizing and moisturizing foundations. If you have skin problems, then you should prefer mineral foundations.

 

How to choose the right foundation color?

Absolutely not in hands! Many people usually check the color of the tonal in their hands and as a result they choose the wrong tone. Both the color and the shape of the inside of your hand, wrist, or arm are different from your face. For this reason, it is better to see the color of the foundation on the face. Checking the foundation you want to choose on a part of your face, chin or neck will help you make a better choice.

 

How to apply foundation?

One of the most widely used and loved options is to apply the foundation to the skin with the help of a brush with light movements. Another popular option is to apply makeup with a sponge. These products will ensure that the foundation is evenly distributed on your skin, and the foundation will absorb well into your skin. It is very important to keep your skin clean and moisturized before applying foundation. To learn more about skin care, we recommend reading our Summer skin care blog.

 

12 types of foundations

Now let's move on to the skin type and the types of foundations according to your skin needs. What types of foundations are there? Here are 12 types of foundation for you. Continue reading to find out which foundation is right for your skin type and which ones will best meet your skin's needs.

 

1) Liquid Foundations

 

 

It is safe to say that liquid foundations are the most popular among makeup users. It is easy to work with and the packaging is very convenient to use. Liquid foundations range from matte to dewy.

 

In most cases, liquid foundations turn into a semi-matte or semi-dewy look after hours on the face - which, of course, is due to the presence of water in the formula. Due to its water-based formula, liquid foundations have moisturizing properties and have a very good effect when used as a daily foundation for people with dry skin type.

 

At the same time, liquid foundations are one of the makeup trends of 2020 with a light structure and moisturizing composition.

 

2) Cream Foundations

 

 

Cream-based foundations give good results on dry skin type. Mostly, cream tones are suitable for cold seasons and mature women, due to the fact that the skin loses more moisture during these periods.

 

Some heavier cream-based tones help cover blemishes and blackheads. Cream tones are produced in the form of glass jars, compact molds and tubes. On the skin, it usually leaves a bright and vibrant look.

 

3) Cream to Powder Foundations

 

 

When cream to powder foundations are applied to the skin, they change from a creamy form to a powdery form. It takes its name from this feature. This transformation helps to create a matte appearance on the skin.

 

Most cream to powder foundations can range from semi-matte to matte. It does not create a feeling of heaviness on the skin, on the contrary, it feels light. It is usually suitable for all skin types, but is irreplaceable for oily skin due to its matte finish.

 

4) Pressed Powder Foundations

 

 

If you have oily skin, pressed powder foundations will be a good choice for you. This is because it has a formula that can cover oily skin and give your skin a more matte look.

 

If you want to use powder foundations alone, apply a primer under your skin before using the powder foundation. Pressed powder foundations have a lighter coverage than other types of foundations, so the makeup base will improve the quality of your skin and also ensure that your makeup lasts longer.

 

5) Mineral Foundations

 

 

Mineral foundations are usually in compact form, pressed or powdered. Due to its rich mineral content, it is a product recommended for allergic and sensitive skin types. The advantage of mineral tones is that it can be used on all skin types. Due to its light structure and closure, it can be easily used in everyday makeup. You can get acquainted with mineral grains offered by many brands on Trendyol website.

 

6) Wipped Foundations

 

 

If you are looking for a fluidity between liquid and cream, you can check out wipped or mousse foundations. Like wipped creams, these foundations leave a feeling of lightness when applied to the skin.

 

Feels like it is weightless, allows you to apply it smoothly and results in a matte look. Good for many skin types, but works great for people with mixed skin types. Depending on your application, the coverage may be moderate or complete.

 

7) Anti-Aging Foundations

 

 

Along with your make-up, anti-aging foundations, which help your skin to live against time, also help skin care with these properties. Depending on the nature of the foundation you choose, this can tighten the skin and reduce wrinkles. Most anti-aging foundations are very fluid and a very small amount can be enough for makeup.

 

The active ingredients in it will help you fight the signs of aging. Make sure that the foundation you choose contains ingredients such as vitamin E, vitamin C, vitamin A, retinol and hyaluronic acid.

 

8) Oil-Based Foundations

Oil-based foundations are perfect for older women and people with dry skin. Provides deep hydration that other makeup foundations.

 

Be careful when using oil-based foundations. After a long stay, the oil will accumulate on the top of the bottle. If the foundation is not shaken and applied properly before use, it will make the skin look oily. Therefore, be sure to shake well before use.

 

9) Waterproof Foundations

 

 

If you traveling or live in hot climates where you sweat a lot, this will erase all your makeup. This does not apply to waterproof foundations.

 

Easily applied to all skin types It comes in both liquid and cream forms and is available in a wide range of colors.

 

Although they do not result in a subtle look like other foundations, they do have countless benefits.

 

10) Stick Foundations

 

 

Stick foundations are an ideal choice to cover skin imperfections. The downside is that it is more difficult to spread evenly on the skin than other foundations. You can also use dark and light tones to reveal contour lines in your makeup.

 

11) BB Creams 

 

 

Perfect for a variety of skin types, BB creams mean "blemish balm" or "beauty balm". BB creams with a creamy formula not only moisturize your skin, but also help to improve skin tone and a number of other skin care forms. If your skin is prone to oily, BB creams with a mattifying effect are just for you.

 

12) CC Creams

 

 

As the name implies, CC creams (color correction) have a balancing effect on skin color. Gives skin natural color & light coverage It usually contains anti-aging ingredients. CC creams can even help create a radiant and fresh look by improving skin tone.

 

BB, CC, DD creams and color moisturizers are not actually foundations. That is, it can be a little behind the creation of grandeur, such as foundations. It is also true that they adapt better to skin color and are better than foundations in terms of moisturizing.

 

 

The search for the perfect foundation is not just about choosing the right color for your skin. You need to find out which formula works best for your skin type and which structural foundation is right for you.
